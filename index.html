Our Concept

Mega Tom’s was founded in 1989 with a basic philosophy to serve great, greasy food with halfway decent service in a fun atmosphere. Founder George Markou had a vision of a restaurant that would offer a simple, original menu featuring gourmet hamburgers, and specialty drinks. In fact, he wanted to recreate the dining experiences he once had. Mega Tom’s Restaurants was an instant hit — we ran out of food the first night at our El Monte, CA (Lower Azusa & Santa Anita) location, and we’ve been building a loyal following ever since. Since that first night, we’ve been slowly expanding and building upon our success. You can now find Mega Toms Restaurants in Murrieta, Ca . Over the years, we’ve added a few more burgers, served too many baskets of our fresh-cut fries to count, and perfected our salads and soft tacos, but our commitment to you – our guest – hasn’t changed.
